# Code of Conduct

LocalHarness adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct. The full text is published at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and help keep this a welcoming,
harassment-free project for everyone — regardless of background or experience
level.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately by
contacting the maintainer through GitHub (a direct message, or the private
[security advisory form](https://github.com/ahwurm/localharness/security/advisories/new)).
All reports are reviewed in confidence and in good faith.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior
and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, commits, and issues
that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any
contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.
